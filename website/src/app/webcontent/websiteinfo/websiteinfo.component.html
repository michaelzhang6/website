Personal Website

The summer after my freshman year at Cornell, I decided to make a website that I could use to keep track of both my academic progress and professional achievements. Being completely new to web development at the time gave me the chance to discover technologies on my own and learn independently. I update the website every few months or so, which helps me keep my web development skills sharp.

This past summer I worked on a project at Raytheon in Angular and subsequently picked up TypeScript/JavaScript. I redesigned my website to be more robust and complex using these skills.

Any feedback on the design or layout of the website is greatly appreciated!
